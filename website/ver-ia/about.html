About VER-IA

VER-IA is a technology company building an operating system for AI-driven business departments. Our platform provides companies with autonomous agents that handle repetitive daily operations — data reporting, team check-ins, collections follow-up, and marketing content production — so their people can focus on decisions, not paperwork.

Our operating principles

  • Human governance by design. Every agent operates under an explicit charter approved by company management. Outward-facing actions (like publishing content) require prior human approval, and every decision is recorded in an auditable journal.
  • Official integrations only. We connect to third-party services — ERPs, CRMs, Microsoft 365, and social platforms such as TikTok — exclusively through their official, documented APIs and authorization flows. We never scrape, reverse-engineer, or automate browsers.
  • Customer ownership. Each customer's data, accounts, and credentials remain theirs. Connections are authorized by the account owner via OAuth and can be revoked at any time.
  • Quality before volume. AI-generated content passes an automated quality gate (accuracy, brand claims, platform standards) before a human ever sees it — and a human always sees it before the world does.

Where we operate

VER-IA is based in Ecuador and currently serves business customers in Latin America, with a platform designed from day one to support multiple companies with strict data isolation between them.
