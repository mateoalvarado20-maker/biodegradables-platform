Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Policy explains what information VER-IA ("we", "us") collects when businesses use our platform (the "Service"), how we protect it, and the choices available to our customers. We build for business customers: we do not sell personal data, we do not serve ads, and we collect only what the Service needs to operate.

1. Information we store

  • Customer account information: company name, names and work email addresses of authorized users, and configuration the customer provides (brand guidelines, business goals, recipients for reports).
  • Business data processed on the customer's behalf: data the customer connects from its own systems (for example ERP sales records) used solely to produce that customer's reports and content.
  • Generated content and decisions: drafts created by the Service, quality-control results, and the customer's approval or rejection decisions (kept as an audit trail).
  • Service logs: technical events needed for reliability, security, and billing.

2. What we obtain through OAuth (including TikTok)

When a customer connects a third-party account (such as TikTok), the connection uses the platform's official OAuth authorization flow: the account owner signs in on the platform's own page and explicitly grants specific permissions. Through that flow we receive and store only:

  • an access token and refresh token issued by the platform, scoped to the permissions granted;
  • the account's basic identifier (such as TikTok's open_id) and the list of granted permissions (for TikTok: user.info.basic and video.publish).

We use these tokens exclusively to publish content that the account owner has approved and to read the basic information needed to operate the connection. We never receive, request, or store the account's password.

3. How we protect tokens and data

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM using keys held separately from the stored data; access requires authenticated, authorized service calls.
  • Each customer's data — including tokens — is isolated per customer; one customer's agents can never read another's data.
  • All data in transit is protected with TLS (HTTPS).
  • Access tokens are short-lived and rotated automatically; if a platform revokes access, we treat the connection as disconnected immediately.
  • Every publication and approval decision is recorded in an auditable journal.

4. How to revoke access

Customers can disconnect a linked account at any time, by either method:

  • From the platform: for TikTok, in the TikTok app go to Settings and privacy → Security & permissions → Apps and services (or the equivalent "Manage app permissions" page) and remove VER-IA. Revocation takes effect immediately.
  • From us: email privacy@ver-ia.com and we will delete the stored tokens for that connection within two business days.

After revocation we no longer can — nor attempt to — act on the account. Stored tokens for revoked or disconnected accounts are deleted.

5. Sharing

We do not sell or rent data. We share data only with the infrastructure providers needed to run the Service (cloud hosting, AI model providers used to generate the customer's own content) under confidentiality obligations, or when required by law.

6. Retention

We keep customer data while the customer relationship is active. Upon termination, or upon request, we delete customer data and OAuth tokens, except minimal records we must keep for legal or accounting purposes.

7. Your rights

Customers and their users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data by writing to privacy@ver-ia.com. We answer within two business days.

8. Changes

If we make material changes to this Policy we will notify customers and update the date at the top of this page.
