Enterprise AI, human-approved

AI agents that run your company's daily operations.

VER-IA is a software-as-a-service platform that gives businesses a team of AI agents for reporting, team coordination, and marketing content — with every outward-facing action reviewed and approved by a human before it happens.

Business intelligence

Daily sales, logistics, and collections reports generated automatically from the company's own ERP and CRM data.

Team coordination

Check-ins, task tracking, and consolidated management summaries delivered through Microsoft Teams.

Marketing content

AI-generated short-form video and carousel content for social media. Every piece passes automated quality control and explicit owner approval before anything is ever published.

How publishing works

VER-IA never posts anything on its own authority. Customers connect their own social media accounts through the platform's official authorization flows (OAuth). Content is drafted by AI, checked by an automated quality gate, and then held for review: the account owner approves or rejects each piece. Only owner-approved content is ever published, and the connection can be revoked by the customer at any time.

VER-IA integrates with third-party platforms exclusively through their official APIs and developer programs. We do not scrape, automate browsers, or take any action outside the scope explicitly authorized by our customers.
